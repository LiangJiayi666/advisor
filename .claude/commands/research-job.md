     1|# /research-job — Job Research
     2|
     3|Parse any JD (job description) the user provides — whether pasted text, a file, or a URL.
     4|
     5|Then activate the `job-research` skill to:
     6|1. Extract key requirements, responsibilities, and qualifications from the JD.
     7|2. Generate a structured job card summarizing the role.
     8|3. Produce a research report covering company context, role positioning, and fit assessment if the user's profile is available.
     9|
    10|If the user has a personal profile or career tendency on file, cross-reference it in the report.
